Item 1.1.5.8.b.

First, notice that

\begin{align*} \mathbf{u}+\mathbf{0} \amp = \mathbf{0}+\mathbf{u} \amp (\knowl{./knowl/axioms-vector-addition-commutativity.html}{\text{Commutativity (1.1.3)}})\\ \amp = \mathbf{u} \amp (\knowl{./knowl/axioms-vector-addition-neutral-elt.html}{\text{Neutral element (1.1.4)}}) \end{align*}

Therefore,

\begin{align*} (\mathbf{u}+\mathbf{0})+(\mathbf{v}+(-\mathbf{u})) \amp = \mathbf{u}+(\mathbf{v}+(-\mathbf{u})) \amp \text{ (above argument)}\\ \amp = \mathbf{u}+((-\mathbf{u})+\mathbf{v}) \amp (\knowl{./knowl/axioms-vector-addition-commutativity.html}{\text{Commutativity (1.1.3)}})\\ \amp = (\mathbf{u}+(-\mathbf{u}))+\mathbf{v} \amp (\knowl{./knowl/axioms-vector-addition-associativity.html}{\text{Associativity (1.1.2)}})\\ \amp = \mathbf{0}+\mathbf{v} \amp (\knowl{./knowl/axioms-vector-addition-inverses.html}{\text{Inverse element (1.1.5)}})\\ \amp = \mathbf{v} \amp (\knowl{./knowl/axioms-vector-addition-neutral-elt.html}{\text{Neutral Element (1.1.4)}}) \end{align*}
