Item 1.1.9.a.
\begin{align*} (k+l)(\mathbf{u}+\mathbf{v}) \amp = (k+l)\mathbf{u}+ (k+l)\mathbf{v} \amp (\knowl{./knowl/axioms-vector-addition-distributivity-1.html}{\text{Distributivity 1 (1.1.8)}})\\ \amp = k\mathbf{u}+l\mathbf{u}+ k\mathbf{v}+l\mathbf{v} \amp (\knowl{./knowl/axioms-vector-addition-distributivity-2.html}{\text{Distributivity 2 (1.1.9)}})\\ \amp = k \mathbf{u} +k \mathbf{v} + l\mathbf{u} + l \mathbf{v} \amp (\knowl{./knowl/axioms-vector-addition-commutativity.html}{\text{Commutativity (1.1.3)}}) \end{align*}

Notice that one could also first have used Distributivity 2 (1.1.9) and only then Distributivity 1 (1.1.8).
