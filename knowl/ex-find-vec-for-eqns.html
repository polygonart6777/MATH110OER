  1. \(\displaystyle 3(2\mathbf{u}+ \mathbf{x}) + \mathbf{w} = 2\mathbf{x} -\mathbf{v}\)
  2. \(2(3\mathbf{v}- \mathbf{x}) = 5\mathbf{w} +\mathbf{u} - 3\mathbf{x}\text{.}\)
