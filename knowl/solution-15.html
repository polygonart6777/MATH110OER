Solution 2.1.5.6.1.

We choose to have all seven vectors anchored at the origin.

Notice that \(\mbf{u}+\mbf{v}\) represents the diagonal of the parallelogram with sides \(\mbf{u}\) and \(\mbf{v}\text{,}\) and similarly, \(-\mbf{u}+\mbf{v}\) represents the diagonal of the parallelogram with sides \(-\mbf{u}\) and \(\mbf{v}\text{.}\) Furthermore, \(-1/2\mbf{v}\) has the opposite direction of \(\mbf{v}\text{,}\) and \(-\mbf{u}\) the opposite direction of \(\mbf{v}\text{.}\)
