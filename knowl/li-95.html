Item 1.2.4.c.
For \(P=( -3 , 5 , 2)\) and \(Q=( 1 , 3 , 3 )\text{,}\) we compute:
\begin{equation*} \vect{PQ} = \begin{bmatrix} 1-(-3) \\ 3-5 \\ 3-2 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 4 \\ 2 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} . \end{equation*}
Therefore,
\begin{equation*} \norm{\vect{PQ}} = \sqrt{4^2 + 2^2 + 1^2} = \sqrt{21}. \end{equation*}
